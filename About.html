About Us

Who we are:

At our website, it’s just the two of us – friends with a shared vision to enhance the world. In a generation enamored with fragrances for their charm, we crafted a platform for everyone to sample a diverse array of scents, helping you find the ideal fragrance for any mood or vibe. Our commitment extends beyond just personal care; we’re passionate about taking care of the world too. That’s why our sample bottles are crafted from sustainable materials like bamboo, reflecting our dedication to both your satisfaction and the planet’s well-being.


Past Projects:

We’ve collaborated with various establishments and schools, emphasizing the significance of contributing to a better and greener Earth. Among these institutions are SP and SIT, where our efforts have been focused on instilling values of environmental responsibility. Together, we’ve worked towards initiatives that not only bring value to these organizations but also foster a collective commitment to sustainable practices. Through these partnerships, we aspire to leave a positive impact on the environment and inspire others to join us in making our planet a healthier and more sustainable home for all
