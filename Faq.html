FAQ

How does the perfume subscription service work? ▼

Our perfume subscription service is simple and convenient. You start by selecting a subscription plan, and each month we'll send you a curated selection of high-quality perfumes right to your doorstep.

What types of perfumes do you offer? ▼

We offer a diverse range of perfumes, including popular brands and a variety of fragrance types such as floral, fruity, woody, and more. You can also customize your preferences based on your scent preferences.

Can I choose the perfumes I receive each month? ▼

Yes, you have full control! Customize your monthly perfume selection based on your preferences and the vibe you're after, ensuring a personalized and enjoyable experience.

Do you offer gift subscriptions? ▼

Yes, we offer gift subscriptions! Surprise your loved ones with the gift of a monthly fragrance experience. Visit our Gift Subscriptions page for more information.

How often will I receive a new perfume? ▼

Our subscription plans offer monthly deliveries. You'll receive a new perfume every month, and you can adjust your preferences or skip a month as needed.
